HYBRID CARS

This project proposes a Hybrid electric car which solves the major problem of fuel and pollution. The basic principle of hybrid car is to use energy that is stored in a battery during and after harnessing it from a solar panel and wind turbine. Power generated by renewal energy sources has recently become one of the most promising solutions for the electrification of islands and remote rural areas. But high dependency on whether conditions and the unpredictable nature of these renewable energy sources are the main drawbacks. To overcome this weakness, different green energy sources (solar and wind) and power electronic converters need to be integrated with each other. The charged batteries are used to drive the motor which serves here as an engine and moves the vehicle in reverse or forward direction. This idea, in future, will help to reduce the fuel consumption and reducing the pollution level as well as minimizing the running cost.

  • Presented project proposal in 7th ALL INDIA EWB Chapters Meet by ENGINEERS WITHOUT BORDERS-INDIA Chennai Professional Chapter on Topic “HYBRID CARS”.
